# Privacy

MLdeck's public privacy positioning is browser-local during normal training flows and privacy-first for CSV-based exploratory modeling.

During normal browser training flows, raw CSV data is not uploaded to MLdeck servers. Backend services may still exist for account, application, or control-plane features.

Users remain responsible for their local browser, device, dataset storage, downloads, exported artifacts, and access controls. Sensitive datasets should be reviewed according to the user's own policies before use.
